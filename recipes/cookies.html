Cookies Recipe

Description:

This is a basic, simple, vegan sugar cookie recipe.

Although I rarely use vegan processed foods that I canâ€™t make in my own kitchen, I experimented with various fats and decided vegan butter worked most consistently for this particular cookie.

Ingredients List:

  • 250 grams Plain Flour
  • 100 grams Vegan Sugar
  • 4 grams Baking Soda
  • 1.5 grams Salt
  • 100 grams Vegan Butter
  • 30 grams Vegan Milk Substitute
  • 4 grams Vanilla Extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/170C Fan/Gas Mark 5/350F
  2. blend the vegan butter and sugar. Then add the vanilla extract and blend another 10 seconds.Shift the flour, baking soda, and salt in bowl.
  3. Add the vegan butter/sugar mixture and the dry ingredients together. Add the vegan milk and mix with hands to form a ball.
  4. Roll the dough between two pieces of parchment paper. Roll to 1/2 centimeter of thickness.
  5. Bake for 6 minutes and leave to cool 5 minutes before removing from tray.
